quiz Frequently Asked Questions

After placing your order, you'll be redirected to the tracking page automatically. You can also find your order under your Profile → Order History. The tracker shows real-time status updates as your order moves from restaurant prep to delivery.
On the checkout page, you'll see a "Promo Code" input field. Enter your code (e.g. SWIFTBITE50) and click "Apply". The discount will be reflected in your order summary immediately.
You can modify or cancel your order while it's still "Pending". Once the restaurant accepts and starts preparing it, modifications are no longer possible. Contact support for urgent changes.
If your order is eligible for a refund, the amount will be returned to your original payment method within 3-5 business days. SwiftBite credits are applied instantly.
Visit the Driver Dashboard to see the driver experience. In a real app, you would register through our Driver Portal, submit required documents, and start receiving delivery assignments in your area.
